# Flipkart Product Sales Analysis Dashboard (2025) | Excel & Power BI
## Project Objective:
The objective of this project is to analyze Flipkart’s product sales data for 2025 using Excel and Power BI to gain actionable insights into sales performance, pricing, discounts, customer ratings, and product distribution across categories, sellers, and return policies, thereby supporting data-driven decision-making and strategic business planning.

## Dataset used
Dataset ="https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/atharvjairath/flipkart-ecommerce-datas"

## Questions 
1.Which categories generate the highest sales revenue?
2.Which sellers contribute most to total sales?
3.What are the top 10 best-selling products?
4.How much revenue comes from each sub-category?

## Key KPIs
Total Sales Value (₹)
Total Units Sold
Average Product Price (₹)
Average Discount (%)
Average Customer Rating
Sales by Category
Sales by Seller
Top-Selling Products
Product Count by Price Range
Return Policy Sales Share (%)
Unsold Product Value (₹)
Category-wise Discount vs Sales
Sub-category Revenue Contribution

## Process
The project began with collecting and cleaning Flipkart sales data using Excel to ensure accuracy and consistency.
The cleaned data was imported into Power BI for data modeling and KPI creation using DAX.
Interactive visuals were designed to analyze sales, pricing, discounts, ratings, and returns. 
Slicers and filters were added to enable dynamic analysis and support data-driven insights.
